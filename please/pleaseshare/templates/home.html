About PleaseShare

Using PleaseShare is easy:

  • You upload a file (or an archive, see the options below);
  • You get a .torrent and a magnet link to share with your friends;
  • Subsequent downloads are at least as fast as a direct HTTP download: this server acts as a webseed for the created torrent.
