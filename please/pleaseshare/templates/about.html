What is pleaseshare?

Pleaseshare is a file-sharing website, with "sharing" as its core value.
It means that there wonâ€™t be a direct link on your file once you upload it, but pleaseshare will generate a torrent for you, with a webseed.
It means that people who download the file will always get the best speed available (since there is a webseed, it will be at worst the same speed than if they downloaded it directly via http).
It also means that the uploader can use this torrent to seed the file that is already on his computer, thus increasing the download speed for people on the other end.

Things to know

Please note that pleaseshare solely relies on DHT, which means that no trackers are needed to get peers. You can provide trackers if you want, but they are not mandatory.
You must also understand that, even if pleaseshare allows you to delete the files that you upload on this server, the torrent can still stay active without a webseed.
